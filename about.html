I love it! Learning to code has been a refreshing challenge, and the most exciting aspect of it is that it never stops changing. I still enjoy all of my hobbies and frequently perform with orchestras and other ensembles whenever I get the chance, but my focus is on leearning new skills and gaining experience in web development.
